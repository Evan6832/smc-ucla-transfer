## Major Notes

Psychology is highly selective. ASSIST notes that transfer students must be admitted as Pre-Psychology to major in Psychology and must complete all preparatory courses listed by the end of spring before transfer. ASSIST also notes that students are strongly encouraged to complete all preparatory courses by the end of fall prior to transfer, and that all courses must be taken for a letter grade.
