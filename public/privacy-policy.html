SMC to UCLA Transfer Planning GPT Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

SMC to UCLA Transfer Planning GPT is an unofficial educational planning assistant that helps users compare Santa Monica College coursework with UCLA major-preparation articulation information.

Information You Provide

Users may upload an unofficial transcript or paste a course list. Users should redact student ID numbers, dates of birth, Social Security numbers, addresses, phone numbers, and other unnecessary personal information before sharing documents.

Use of External Services

This GPT uses custom Actions to read public ASSIST articulation data and, when authorized by the user, read Google Drive source documents. Google Drive access is read-only and is used only to retrieve curated articulation reference documents.

Data Storage

This GPT does not intentionally store transcripts, grades, or student course histories in an external database controlled by this GPT. It does not write user transcript data to Google Drive.

Unofficial Guidance

Outputs are unofficial planning aids and are not official academic advising, transfer certification, admission evaluation, or enrollment guidance. Users should verify all transfer plans with an SMC counselor before making enrollment or application decisions.

Contact

For questions about this assessment/demo GPT, contact the GPT creator through the channel where the GPT was shared.
